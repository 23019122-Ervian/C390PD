Garage Getaway

A game project created during one of my modules

Game Details:
  • Genre: Science Adventure
  • Platform: VR
  • Development Time: 3 Months
  • Key Features: The unique selling point for this game is it’s in a Garage setting. Apart from that the puzzles are quite interesting to me and if further developed, could have further potential.

The game would likely take 1 hours for the player to fully immerse in the VR experience. Players must solve a series of four interconnected puzzles to ultimately unlock the garage door and escape. Each puzzle builds on the previous one
